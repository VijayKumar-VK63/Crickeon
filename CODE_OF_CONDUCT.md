# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (doxing)
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on any protected characteristic

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing `hello@crickeon.app` with:

- A clear description of the incident
- Names/usernames involved
- When and where it occurred
- Any relevant context

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Reporters will be informed of outcomes.

### Consequences

Community members who violate this code of conduct will be subject to:

1. **First offense**: Written warning and request for apology
2. **Repeated offense**: Temporary suspension from repository/community
3. **Severe or repeated offenses**: Permanent ban

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, issues, pull requests, or ban contributors as needed to maintain a safe community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the Crickeon project:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Official communication channels (Twitter, Discord, email)
- Code repositories
- Project team interactions
- Community events (online or in-person)
